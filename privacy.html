📋 This Privacy Policy explains how CalcHub collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our free calculator tools.
ℹ️

Information We Collect

CalcHub is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not require any registration or personal information to use our tools.

We may automatically collect limited non-personal information including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile or desktop)
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Time and date of your visit
  • General geographic location (country/region)
🔒

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used solely to improve our services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Analyze which tools are most popular
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand how visitors use our site

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

🍪

Cookies

CalcHub may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences.

We may use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies — to understand website traffic
  • Preference cookies — to remember your settings
  • Advertising cookies — used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

📢

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: google.com/policies/privacy/partners

📊

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as page views, session duration, and traffic sources.

This data helps us improve our tools and user experience. Google Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information.

🛡️

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data transmission between your browser and our servers.

All calculator inputs you enter (such as age, weight, income) are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to or stored on our servers.

👶

Children's Privacy

CalcHub does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our services are designed for general audiences. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

🔗

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

📝

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of CalcHub after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
